Our work was on space exploration, during the Science, Technology and Society (UENR 202) course in university of Energy and Natural resources. As we researched which project to work on, we saw the need to somehow merge medical Laboratory with space exploration but it was quite difficult to find something to do since the internet produce very little results for our work. Instead of taking infomation from the internet, we decided to contribute to the internet by creating a website on the past, present and future on space biology.
